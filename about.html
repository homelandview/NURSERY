About Us

Founded in 2020, GreenLeaf Nursery is dedicated to enriching urban and rural environments with high-quality, affordable plants. Our mission is to make greenery accessible to everyone. We grow and sell a variety of species including flowering plants, succulents, herbs, and trees.

With a focus on sustainability and customer care, we aim to spread the joy of gardening one plant at a time.
